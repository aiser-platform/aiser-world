📊 Create Your First Chart

Experience the magic of AI-powered analytics in just 2 minutes!

This guide will walk you through creating your first chart using Aiser Platform's AI capabilities. You'll see how natural language queries transform into beautiful, insightful visualizations.

🎯 What You'll Learn​

  • ✅ Upload data to Aiser Platform
  • ✅ Ask questions in natural language
  • ✅ Generate charts with AI assistance
  • ✅ Customize visualizations to your needs
  • ✅ Export and share your insights

📁 Sample Data​

We'll use the included sample data file: demo_sales_data.csv

Data Overview:

  • Product sales across different regions
  • Time series data for trend analysis
  • Multiple dimensions for rich insights
  • Realistic business data for meaningful analysis

🚀 Step-by-Step Guide​

Step 1: Access the Platform​

  1. Open your browser and navigate to http://localhost:3000
  2. Sign in with your credentials (or create an account)
  3. Navigate to the main dashboard

Step 2: Upload Your Data​

  1. Click the "Upload Data" button or drag-and-drop area
  2. Select the demo_sales_data.csv file from your project directory
  3. Wait for the AI to analyze and process your data
  4. Review the data preview and schema detection

💡 AI Insight: Aiser automatically detects data types, relationships, and suggests analysis opportunities.

Step 3: Ask Your First Question​

Type your question in natural language:

"Show me sales by month"

What happens behind the scenes:

  1. AI Analysis: Natural language processing understands your intent
  2. Query Generation: Converts to optimized database queries
  3. Chart Selection: AI chooses the best chart type for your data
  4. Visualization: Renders a beautiful, interactive chart

Step 4: Explore Your Chart​

Your generated chart will include:

  • Interactive elements (zoom, pan, hover)
  • Responsive design that works on all devices
  • Professional styling with consistent branding
  • Export options (PNG, PDF, CSV)

🧪 Try These Sample Questions​

📈 Trend Analysis​

"Show me sales trends over the last 6 months"
"What's the growth rate of our top products?"
"Display revenue by quarter with year-over-year comparison"

🥧 Distribution Analysis​

"Create a pie chart of sales by region"
"What's the market share of each product category?"
"Show me the distribution of order values"

🔍 Comparative Analysis​

"Compare sales performance between regions"
"What's the correlation between price and quantity sold?"
"Show me top 10 products by revenue vs. units sold"

📊 Advanced Insights​

"Identify seasonal patterns in our sales data"
"What are the key drivers of revenue growth?"
"Create a heatmap of sales by region and time period"

Styling Options​

  1. Color Schemes: Choose from professional palettes
  2. Fonts & Typography: Customize text appearance
  3. Layout: Adjust spacing and positioning
  4. Annotations: Add notes and highlights

🔄 Iterative Refinement​

The AI learns from your feedback:

  1. Ask follow-up questions to refine your analysis
  2. Modify chart parameters based on AI suggestions
  3. Save your preferences for future use
  4. Build on previous insights with contextual memory

📤 Exporting & Sharing​

Export Formats​

  • 📊 PNG/JPEG: High-resolution images for presentations
  • 📄 PDF: Professional reports and documentation
  • 📈 CSV/Excel: Data for further analysis
  • 🔗 Interactive HTML: Embed in websites and dashboards

Sharing Options​

  • Direct Link: Share with team members
  • Embed Code: Integrate into existing systems
  • API Access: Programmatic access to your charts
  • Dashboard Integration: Combine multiple charts

AI-Powered Insights​

  • Automated anomaly detection
  • Predictive analytics and forecasting
  • Natural language explanations of trends
  • Actionable recommendations based on data

Collaboration Tools​

  • Real-time collaboration on charts

🎯 Best Practices​

Writing Effective Queries​

  1. Be specific: "Show me sales by region" vs. "Show me data"
  2. Include context: "Compare Q1 vs Q4 sales by product category"
  3. Specify timeframes: "Last 12 months" vs. "all time"
  4. Request specific chart types: "Create a heatmap of..."

Data Preparation​

  1. Clean your data before uploading
  2. Use consistent naming conventions
  3. Include metadata for better AI understanding
  4. Validate data quality for accurate insights

🐛 Troubleshooting​

Common Issues​

Chart not generating:

  • Check data format and structure
  • Verify AI service is running
  • Review error messages in browser console

Unexpected results:

  • Clarify your question with more detail
  • Check data quality and completeness
  • Use the AI suggestions for better queries

Performance issues:

  • Reduce dataset size for testing
  • Check system resources
  • Use appropriate chart types for large datasets
