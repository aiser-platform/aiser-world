## 🚀 Demo Walkthrough

### Phase 1: Data Upload & AI Analysis ⏱️ 2 minutes

#### Step 1: Upload Your Data

1. **Navigate** to the main dashboard
2. **Click** "Upload Data" or drag-and-drop the CSV file
3. **Watch** as AI analyzes your data structure

**🎯 What to observe:**
- AI automatically detects data types
- Schema relationships are identified
- Data quality insights are provided
- Analysis suggestions appear

#### Step 2: AI Data Understanding

**The AI will show you:**
```
🔍 Data Analysis Complete!

📊 Dataset Overview:
- 1,247 records | 8 columns | 5 dimensions
- Time range: Jan 2024 - Dec 2024
- Data quality: 98.5% complete

💡 Suggested Analysis:
- Seasonal trends in sales
- Regional performance comparison
- Product category insights
- Customer segmentation analysis
```

### Phase 2: Your First AI-Generated Chart ⏱️ 3 minutes

#### Step 3: Natural Language Query

**Type this question:**
```
"Show me monthly sales trends for the last year"
```

**🎯 Watch the AI in action:**
1. **Query Understanding**: AI parses your intent
2. **Data Processing**: Optimized queries are generated
3. **Chart Selection**: AI chooses a line chart for trends
4. **Visualization**: Beautiful, interactive chart appears

#### Step 4: Explore Your Chart

**Your generated chart includes:**
- 📈 **Trend Line**: Clear monthly sales progression
- 🎨 **Professional Styling**: Consistent with your brand
- 🔍 **Interactive Elements**: Hover for details, zoom to explore
- 📊 **Insights Panel**: AI-generated observations and recommendations

### Phase 3: Advanced Analytics ⏱️ 5 minutes

#### Step 5: Comparative Analysis

**Ask for deeper insights:**
```
"Compare sales performance between regions and show me the top 3"
```

**🎯 AI Response:**
- **Chart Type**: Bar chart with regional comparison
- **Insights**: "North America leads with 42% of total sales"
- **Recommendations**: "Focus marketing efforts in Asia-Pacific (15% growth)"

#### Step 6: Correlation Discovery

**Explore relationships:**
```
"What's the correlation between product price and sales volume?"
```

**🎯 AI Analysis:**
- **Chart Type**: Scatter plot with trend line
- **Insights**: "Strong negative correlation (-0.73) between price and volume"
- **Business Value**: "Premium products have lower volume but higher margins"

### Phase 4: Predictive Insights ⏱️ 3 minutes

#### Step 7: Forecasting

**Ask about the future:**
```
"Predict sales for the next quarter based on current trends"
```

**🎯 AI Prediction:**
- **Forecast Chart**: Line chart with confidence intervals
- **Key Factors**: Seasonal patterns, growth rate, market conditions
- **Risk Assessment**: "High confidence (87%) in Q1 2025 forecast"

#### Step 8: Anomaly Detection

**Identify unusual patterns:**
```
"Are there any anomalies in our sales data?"
```

**🎯 AI Detection:**
- **Anomaly Chart**: Highlighted unusual data points
- **Explanation**: "December spike due to holiday season (expected)"
- **Action Items**: "Investigate March dip in Asia-Pacific region"
